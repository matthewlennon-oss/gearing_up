Year 7 simple machines

Wheels and Axles

Today you will learn how a wheel and axle can make a task easier by changing force, distance or speed.

Learning Intentions

By the end of this lesson, you should be able to identify the wheel and axle, explain how they turn together, and use simulation results to describe force and speed multiplication.

Lesson Tasks

  1. Watch the video your teacher has attached in Google Classroom.
  2. Open the bookwork notes and copy the short notes into your science book.
  3. Open the simulator. Complete Force Lab, Speed Lab and the Design Challenge.
  4. Complete the worksheet using results from the simulator.
  5. Finish with the exit response at the end of the worksheet.
